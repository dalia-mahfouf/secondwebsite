Why apply to La Squadra?

La Squadra is a club created in June 2020 by a group of students of HEC Paris who want to help you find the perfect address for the perfect meal.

About us

"I created La Squadra to bring HEC students together around what I like best: eating. As a brunch lover, every weekend I used to post my favorite addresses in Paris on Instagram and I was always asked where to organize a date, a family dinner... That's where the adventure began. Since then, La Squadra has grown and so have our values. Conviviality of course, but also creativity" - LSQ's President
